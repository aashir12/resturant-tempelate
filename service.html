NVOCC

Seaside Shipping operates as an agent for NVOCC (Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier) providing slot arrangements with all feeder operators and few main line operators.
Our principles fleet consists of 20’HD, 40’HC, and special equipment like 20’/40’ Open Top, 20’/40’ Flat Racks, 45’ and Reefer containers, having services between Indian Subcontinent, Middle East, Southeast Asia & Far East ports.
As a Multimodal Transport Operator, we have services to all major ports and ICD locations
